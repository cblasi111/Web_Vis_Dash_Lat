Latitude vs. Humidity

The relationship between Humidty and the proximity to the equator shows less signifigance than temperature. However, there is something to be seen. You will notice a higher concentration of high humidity near the 0 Degree Latitude mark. But we cannot infer that other variables aren't adding to high humidty because there exist locations far from the equator that also experience higher humidty levels. Humidity depends on both the water vapor content and the air temperature. And the reason humidity is predominantly higher near the equator is because the actual vapor pressure is high.
